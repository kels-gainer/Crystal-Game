CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

The object of the puzzle is to try to add points to equal that of the random number displayed.

The four crystal cards below are each assigned a random number which, when clicked in the right order, will allow you to add a specific amount of points to your total score. When your number matches that of the random number you win, if it goes over then you lose. Each time the game starts the value of the crystals and the random number will change.
